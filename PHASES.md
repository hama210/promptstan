# PromptStan Roadmap

## Completed

1. **Brand and product foundation** — Kurdish-first identity, multilingual UI, prompt library concept.
2. **Architecture and data** — React structure, Cloudflare Worker API, D1 schema, categories, tags, counters.
3. **Admin and publishing** — protected admin dashboard, prompt create/edit/delete, R2 uploads, daily publishing.
4. **AI Before/After automation** — FLUX.2 image generation/editing, real R2 image storage, synchronous publishing, public image verification.

## Current

5. **Launch and growth**
   - ✅ Shareable `/prompt/:slug` pages
   - ✅ WhatsApp, Telegram, and social preview metadata
   - ✅ SEO, structured data, robots rules, and dynamic sitemap
   - ✅ Empty-library fallback pages and D1 starter seed
   - ⏳ Public navigation and conversion improvements
   - ⏳ Share analytics and growth loops

## Next

6. **Content scale** — larger prompt rotation, collections, scheduling controls, duplicate prevention, quality scoring.
7. **Analytics and growth loops** — traffic dashboard, top searches, share tracking, referral links.
8. **Accounts and monetization** — optional sign-in, synced favorites, premium collections, sponsored placements.
